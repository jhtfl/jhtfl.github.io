Instruction to play SMTV in tiles on Windows/Linux/MacOS platform

  • Please refer to SMTV Livestream Tiling for indepth details.
  • Here's the video resolutions list played on Youtube SMTV channel: smtv_resolution.txt
  • Download the latest releases of ffmpeg and yt-dlp and put them on your PATH.
  • Create a script file for Windows or Linux/MacOS
  • Launch the scirpt with parameters to specify the column number and row number. For example: "smtv 4 3" which will play SMTV in tiles as 4 columns and 3 rows.
